Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Bite ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal calorie and macro tracking app. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

1. What data we collect

  • Email address – collected when you sign up via email one-time password or Google OAuth, used solely for authentication.
  • Food and nutrition data – food items you create, calorie and macro values, and daily log entries. This is the core data of the app. It is created entirely by you.
  • Photos – when you use the "Snap & Log" feature, a photo is sent to our backend API for AI analysis. Photos are not stored; they are transmitted, analysed, and discarded immediately.
  • App settings and goals – your daily macro goals and preferences (e.g. time format, theme) are stored to sync across devices.

2. How data is stored

All user data (account, food database, log entries, settings) is stored in Supabase, a managed Postgres database service. Data is protected by Row Level Security — each user can only access their own data. Supabase infrastructure is hosted on AWS.

Photo analysis requests are processed by our API hosted on Microsoft Azure (Australia East region) and by Anthropic's Claude API. No photo data is retained after the response is returned.

3. How data is used

Your data is used exclusively to provide the Bite app's features. We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share your data with anyone except the infrastructure providers listed above (under their own privacy policies)
  • Store or analyse your food photos

4. Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below. We will permanently delete your account and all data within 30 days.

5. Children

Bite is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
